DerivationSet

public struct DerivationSet
extension Forest.DerivationSet: Collection

A subset of the parses of a single symbol over a range of source positions.

Could also be seen as a notional iterator over that subset (which starts out being the whole set of such parses), via repeated calls to removeFirst.
