Grammar

public struct Grammar<StoredSymbol> where StoredSymbol : FixedWidthInteger, StoredSymbol : SignedInteger

A collection of Backus-Naur Form (BNF) rules, each defining a symbol on its left-hand side in terms of a string of symbols on its right-hand side.
